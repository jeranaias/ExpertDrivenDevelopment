Resources

Curated prompts, downloadable templates, answers to common questions, and reference materials to support your Expert-Driven Development projects.

Whether you are starting your first AI-assisted build or refining your workflow, these resources provide practical tools you can use immediately. Each resource is drawn directly from the EDD training curricula and SOP.

Curated, copy-ready prompts organized by use case: admin tools, training aids, data analysis, and reporting. Each prompt is tested and ready for M365 Copilot or Azure OpenAI.

Course Catalog

Expert-Driven Development training is structured as a progressive series. Start with the required Orientation, then build real tools in Platform Training, and sharpen your skills in the Advanced Workshop. Supervisors complete a separate 30-minute orientation.

Required

Training 1: AI-Fluent Development Orientation

Learn the foundational mindset and prompting techniques for working with AI development tools. Covers the Prompt Formula, iteration strategies, and two live build demonstrations.

Duration
2 hours
Audience
All developers
Outcome
Write effective prompts and iterate on AI output
View Course
Recommended

Training 2: Platform Training

Hands-on workshop building three real tools on the Power Platform: an equipment tracker, a request workflow, and a leadership dashboard. Includes troubleshooting patterns.

Duration
4 hours
Audience
Developers with Orientation complete
Outcome
Build functional apps, workflows, and dashboards
View Course
Optional

Training 3: Advanced Workshop

Architect multi-app solutions, build role-based screens, implement advanced automation patterns, debug hard problems, and learn to teach others the EDD methodology.

Duration
4 hours
Audience
Experienced EDD practitioners
Outcome
Design complex solutions and mentor others
View Course
Required for Supervisors

Supervisor Orientation

A concise overview for supervisors covering what EDD is and isn't, time allocation expectations, how to evaluate problem definitions, your role in the workflow, and where to find help.

Duration
30 minutes
Audience
Supervisors and leadership
Outcome
Support and evaluate EDD efforts effectively
